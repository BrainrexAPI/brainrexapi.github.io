BrainRex Makes Crypto Data Simple

BrainRex is an single API for collecting, transforming, and routing logs, metrics, and events from crypto networks and markets.

Get StartedCreate Free Account

#Features

Anomaly Detection

Built in Lambda, BrainRex is blistering fast and memory efficient. It detects anomalies without supervision or need for labeled data.

Language Understanding

We are teaching AI everything about crypto. Sentiment and price sentiment can sense entity analysis in mind.

Integrations

BrainRex unifies crypto, blockchain, and cloud providers at the source, making it easy to collect and ship all observability data.

Runs on Premise

We have partnered IBM, deploying as an agent or service.

Dashboards and alerts

Programmable transforms give you the full power of a alerts. Handle complex use cases with ease!

Trading Backstesting

BrainRex is clear on it's guarantees, helping you to make the appropriate trade offs for your use case.
